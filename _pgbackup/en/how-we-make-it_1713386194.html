How do we make it?

From generation to generation we have shared the experience and knowledge of our land. For us, making mezcal is a passion, a profession from which we seek consistency, nobility, and perfection.

01Field

We have our espadín agave fieldslocated in Tlacolula Oaxaca, being the most typicalagave in the region.

Our master mezcalier takes care of the agave plant since it is a seed and until it reaches it maduration stage, wich takes from 6 to 8 years.

02Cooking

We cook espadín agave pineapples in stone ovens, achieving the perfect balance between soft smoked notes and the pure flavor of agave in a traditional way.

03Distillation

Once cooked and distilled, the perfect harmony between two distillations with different intensities and flavor-aroma profiles is achieved.

04 Aging

To age Señorío reposado and Señorío añejo labels, we use American white oak barrels which gives them more complex flavor notes.
